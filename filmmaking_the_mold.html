The mold
filmmaking
2023
I supervised the cinematography for this short film that was written, directed, and produced by Giuseppe Venturini. The mold (La muffa) is a story about sorrow and rebirth. "As a mysterious mold grows on the walls of his bedroom, a young man's conditions get worse and worse."
