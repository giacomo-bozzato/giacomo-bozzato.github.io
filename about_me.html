about me
Hi! I'm 23 and come from Venice, Italy. I'd define myself as an ambitious and eclectic explorer who strives to combine creativity with analysis. When designing, I give my best in the phase of concept definition because I like to see the big picture and to think about the profound goals of a project. I've a strong passion for cinema but my interests include also natural sciences, philosophy, semiotics, and anthropology. Now I'm concluding my master's degree at the Polytechnic School of Milan after spending a semester in Paris at ECV. Currently I'm developing my research thesis about
post-anthropocentric design, digital media, and sustainable businesses. I feel truly concerned about the environment and I strongly believe that good design comes from reasoned ethical principles, for the sake of human and non-human living beings. Generally I pursue the values of honesty and minimalism both in my life and in the projects I work on. I'm an eager reader, love to play basketball, and play the piano. Finally, yes, I genuinely believe that design can contribute a little bit to make this world a better place, in a glocal perspective. At least, this is why I do design! 🌱
