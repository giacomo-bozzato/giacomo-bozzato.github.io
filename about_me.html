about me
Hi! I'm Giacomo, a multimedia designer focused on UX/UI and photography. Trained in Italy at the Polytechnic School of Milan, I spent a semester in Paris, and currently I'm based in Sydney. I work across digital interfaces and content creation, with a focus on visual storytelling for food, sustainability, and conscious businesses.
My background includes branding, filmmaking, creative direction, and research. Over the years,
I've worked on digital products, campaigns, and photo/film production for startups, global fintechs, and independent creative projects across Europe and Australia.
My design approach is more-than-human centered and aims for honesty and minimalism, combining strategy with creativity. I care about feeling and meaning. I believe that good design is both functional, beautiful, and ethical. 🌱
